Student Housing Dining

SPIS students will live in Marshall Residential Halls N, O, or S in Marshall College.

Check-in (9 am - 12 pm on Sunday, August 4, 2019) will be at the ERC Conference Desk. Check-out (7:30 am - 9 am and 1:30 pm - 3:30 pm on Friday, September 6, 2019) will be at the ERC Conference Desk.

Participants may check-out early, but will need to communicate specific times prior to August 26th to Cyndi Meeves (Hospitality and Conference Services, 858-531-6625). The following information will help you locate the ERC Conference Desk and park at Pangea Parking Structure.

2019 SPIS Housing FAQs: This document will provide additional details pertaining to your stay during SPIS.

Students will have the option of dining at one of three dining facilities during the five week stay. First meal will begin with dinner on August 4th and the last meal will be lunch on September 6th.

The three dining locations are: Café Ventanas, Pines, or 64 Degrees. Please note that meal cards will only be valid for one swipe per meal period. Operating hours are 6:30 am - 8:00 pm.

  • Location: Cafe Ventanas
    • The following time blocks are assigned to SPIS students for each day of the week:
      • Breakfast: 7:15-8:00 am
      • Lunch: 12:00-12:45 pm
      • Dinner: 6:30-7:15 pm
  • Location: Pines
  • Location: 64 Degrees

Café Ventanas is the home dining facility for SPIS Students. Participants are able to eat at Pines or 64 Degrees, however, be aware that those dining facilities may have large groups already assigned. A capacity breakdown by time block for Café Ventanas and available dining locations (as some may close) will be posted on the Monday of each week to facilitate your planning.

The Village Market will be available if students would like to eat there (cash, credit card or Triton Cash). The Village Market is not part of the dining plan.

Special Meal Accomodations

For any special meal accommodations, the Medical E Form will need to be completed for the students that don't feel they can manage their dietary needs with the options that will be already be presented in the unit. Please complete the form and bring it with you if you need any special meal accommodations.
